Sustainable Air Cushion Utilization on Mars

Air cushions can be repurposed from packaging waste into essential structural components. By using local Martian regolith (soil), we can efficiently convert these plastic remnants into dense, heavy ballasts. This method avoids relying on Earth-based resources for stability, achieving a substantial 12x mass increase: 10 kg of cushion material yields up to 120 kg of stable ballast.
Air cushions are made of Polyethylene.
Difficulty factor: 1/3
