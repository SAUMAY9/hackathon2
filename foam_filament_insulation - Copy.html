Sustainable Foam Packaging Reuse on Mars

After inflating the habitat on Mars, leftover foam packaging can be repurposed as lightweight thermal insulation. By cutting or shredding the foam and layering it inside walls, floors, or around pipes, the habitat maintains stable temperatures, protecting the crew from extreme Martian cold and reducing energy usage. This approach turns space waste into a practical resource for sustainable living on Mars.
