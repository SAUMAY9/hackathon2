Sustainable Carbon Fiber Recycling on Mars

Astronauts on Mars face limited resources and accumulating carbon fiber waste from cargo shipments and rover parts. This process provides a sustainable solution by transforming discarded carbon fiber into 3D printing filament for habitat use. Through collection, shredding, densification, pellet extrusion, purification, and filament extrusion, the recycled material is converted into functional items such as replacement parts and modular furniture.
